My Plants

Peace Lily

Scientific Name: Spathiphyllum wallisii
Soil: Moist, well-draining
Light: bright, filtered, indirect light
Water: Keep soil moist
Temperature: 65 to 80
Safety: Toxic to animals and humans

Money Tree

Scientific Name: Pachira aquatica
Soil: Sandy, peat-moss-based soil
Light: Bright, indirect light
Water: Water until water runs out of the bottom of the pot every one to two weeks
Temperature: 60 to 75 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Nontoxic

Woolly Cactus

Scientific Name:Espostoa guentheri
Soil: Fast-draining
Light: 3-4 hour of direct sunlight
Water: Wait until soil is completely dry.
Temperature: 65 to 75 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Toxic to animals
