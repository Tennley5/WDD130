Burro's Tail

Scientific Name: Schlumbergera buckleyi
Soil: Dry, sandy soil
Light: Bright light or partial sun for 4+ hours
Water: Once every 2 weeks
Temperature: 65 to 80 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Mildly toxic to animals and humans if ingested

Jade Plant

Scientific Name: Crassula ovata
Soil: Loose, rocky well-draining soil.
Light: bright light for 6+ hours
Water: Once every 2 to 3 weeks.
Temperature: 65 to 75 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Mildly toxic to humans and animals.

Aloe Vera

Scientific Name: Aloe Vera
Soil: Loose, rocky well-draining soil
Light: bright, indirect light for 6 to 8 hours a day
Water: Once per week
Temperature: 55 to 85 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Toxic if ingested
