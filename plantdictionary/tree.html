Dragon Tree

Scientific Name: Dracaena marginata
Soil: Loamy, well-draining soil
Light: Filtered bright, indoor light
Water: When soil feels dry (Once a week)
Temperature: 70 to 80 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Toxic to pets

Corn Plant

Scientific Name:Dracaena fragrans
Soil: Loose, loamy potting soil
Light: indirect sunlight
Water: Wait until soil is completely dry.
Temperature: 65 to 80 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Toxic to animals

Money Tree

Scientific Name: Pachira aquatica
Soil: Sandy, peat-moss-based soil
Light: Bright, indirect light
Water: Water until water runs out of the bottom of the pot every one to two weeks
Temperature: 60 to 75 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Nontoxic
