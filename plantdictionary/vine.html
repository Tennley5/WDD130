Pothos

Scientific Name: Epipremnum aureum
Soil: Fast-draining
Light: Low to bright indirect light
Water: Water when the soil is dry
Temperature: 65 to 80 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Toxic to pets

Spiderwort

Scientific Name: Tradescantia
Soil: Fast-draining
Light: Bright indirect light
Water: Water once the top inch of soil is dry
Temperature: 65 to 75 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Toxic to animals and humans
