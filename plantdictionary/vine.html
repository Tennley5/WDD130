Pothos

Scientific Name: Epipremnum aureum
Soil: Well-draining
Light: Bright, inderect light
Water: Every 1-2 weeks
Temperature: 70 to 90 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Toxic to pets

Spiderwort

Scientific Name: Tradescantia
Soil: moisture retaining soil
Light: Bright indirect light
Water: Water once the top inch of soil is dry (once a week)
Temperature: 60 to 80 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Toxic to cats

Heartleaf Philodendron

Scientific Name: Philodendron hederaceum
Soil: moist,well-draining
Light: medium, indirect light
Water: Every 1 to 2 weeks
Temperature: 65 to 80 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Toxic to animals
