Peace Lily

Scientific Name: Spathiphyllum wallisii
Soil: Fast-draining
Light:10-14 hours of indirect sunlight
Water: Keep soil moist
Temperature: 60 to 85
Safety: Poisonous if eaten or chewed on.

Anthurium

Scientific Name:Anthurium andraeanum
Soil: Fast-draining
Light: Medium to bright light
Water: Keep soil evenly moist
Temperature: 65 to 80 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: It is poisonous and can cause illness if eaten or chewed

Crown-of-Thorns

Scientific Name: Euphorbia milii
Soil: Fast-draining
Light: Bright to intense light
Water: keep soil moderately dry
Temperature: 65 to 75 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: This plant is poisonous and the milky sap can cause illness or skin irritation if eaten or chewed by children or pets
