Peace Lily

Scientific Name: Spathiphyllum wallisii
Soil: Moist, well-draining
Light: bright, filtered, indirect light
Water: Keep soil moist
Temperature: 65 to 80
Safety: Toxic to animals and humans

Anthurium

Scientific Name:Anthurium andraeanum
Soil: loose, well-draining soil
Light: bright, indirect light
Water: six ice cubes or a half cup of water once a week
Temperature: 78 to 90 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Toxic

Crown-of-Thorns

Scientific Name: Euphorbia milii
Soil: Dry to medium moisture, well-drained soil
Light: Bright, direct sunlight for 3 to 4 hours daily
Water: When soil feels dry
Temperature: 65 to 75 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Highly Toxic
