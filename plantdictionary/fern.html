Boston Fern

Scientific Name: Nephrolepis exaltata
Soil: Loamy, well-draining
Light:10-14 hours of indirect sunlight
Water: Wait until soil feels dry (3-5 days)
Temperature: 65 to 80 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Nontoxic

Rabbit's Foot Fern

Scientific Name: Phlebodium aureum
Soil: Standard potting soil, sand, and peat moss mix
Light: Bright, indirect light
Water: Wait until soil is completely dry.
Temperature: 65 to 75 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Toxic to animals

Lady Fern

Scientific Name: Athyrium filix-femina
Soil: Well-draining potting soil
Light: Bright, indirect light.
Water: 1 cup of water every 2 weeks
Temperature: 70 to 100 degrees Fahrenheit
Safety: Nontoxic
